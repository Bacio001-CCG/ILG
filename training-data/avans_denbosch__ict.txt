## BUSINESS IT AND MANAGEMENT – ICT SPECIALIZATION

**Location:** Den Bosch
**Start month:** September
**Duration:** 4 years, full-time Bachelor’s
**Language:** Dutch

---

## ABOUT THE PROGRAM

Business IT and Management (BIM) is a specialization within the HBO ICT program. You work at the intersection of business and IT and learn how to improve organizations using smart technologies such as data analysis, AI, and modern information systems.

In this program, you learn how organizations operate and how IT can support and improve business processes. You analyze problems within companies, design data-driven solutions, and help implement them. Sometimes you develop software yourself, and sometimes you guide developers and stakeholders in realizing the solution. You are the link between business needs and technical possibilities.

During the program, you work on real-life assignments from companies and organizations. You collaborate in teams, communicate with clients, and learn how to advise organizations in their digital transformation.

---

## WHY BUSINESS IT AND MANAGEMENT?

* You combine business knowledge with IT expertise.
* You learn to use data and AI to improve organizations.
* You work on real projects from practice.
* You develop strong communication and advisory skills.
* You receive personal guidance from lecturers with professional experience.

---

## WHAT ARE YOU TRAINED FOR?

The Business IT and Management specialization trains you to become a professional who understands both organizational processes and IT systems. You learn how to analyze information flows, translate business questions into IT solutions, and manage digital projects.

You are educated to act as a connector between management, users, and IT specialists. Your role is often advisory and coordinating, with a strong focus on data, systems, and innovation.

---

## POSSIBLE PROFESSIONS AFTER THIS PROGRAM

* **Information Analyst:** You analyze business processes and information flows and propose improvements.
* **BI / Data Consultant:** You help organizations turn data into insights and knowledge.
* **IT Project Manager:** You manage IT projects and guide teams toward successful implementation.
* **AI Business Consultant:** You translate organizational needs into AI-driven solutions.
* **Business IT Consultant:** You advise organizations on how to use IT more effectively.
* **Process Improvement Specialist:** You optimize business processes using IT systems.

---

## WHAT IS EXPECTED OF YOU?

You choose Business IT and Management because you are curious about how organizations work and how technology can make them better. You enjoy analyzing problems, thinking creatively, and working with data. You are communicative, like to collaborate, and can switch between technical and non-technical perspectives.

You are interested in both people and technology and want to act as a bridge between them. Prior programming knowledge is not required.

---

## FURTHER EDUCATION

After completing the Business IT and Management specialization, you can continue your studies with a 1 or 2-year Master’s program at a university of applied sciences or a research university.

Suitable Master’s programs at Avans include:

* Chain Information Systems
* Strategic Information Management
* ERP and Logistics

You can also choose the HBO Top Program transfer minor, which allows you to start a university Master’s program more quickly.

## CURRICULUM (DEN BOSCH)

**Study structure**
You work in periods of 10 weeks on a central theme. Each period includes a group project supported by courses and practical assignments. You also develop professional skills such as communication, project management, and research.

**Year 1**
Introduction to IT and business processes. You learn how organizations use IT and start working on real company cases.
Important subjects include:

* Business Administration
* Information & Process Analysis
* Databases
* Communication and Reporting

**Year 2**
You deepen your knowledge of information systems and data. You work with ERP systems, Business Intelligence, and low-code development.
Important subjects include:

* ERP
* Business Intelligence
* Low-Code Development
* Programming

**Year 3**
You complete a half-year internship and focus on data analytics. You learn how to translate organizational problems into data-driven solutions.
Important components:

* Internship
* Data Analytics
* Elective module

**Year 4**
You graduate by working independently on an IT-related business problem. You choose your own graduation topic and complete a final project and elective module.
