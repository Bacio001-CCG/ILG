
## BUSINESS IT AND MANAGEMENT – ICT SPECIALIZATION

**Location:** Den Bosch
**Start month:** September
**Duration:** 4 years, full-time Bachelor’s
**Language:** Dutch

---

## ABOUT THE PROGRAM

Business IT and Management (BIM) is a specialization within the HBO ICT program. You work at the intersection of business and IT and learn how organizations can be improved using smart technologies such as data analysis and artificial intelligence.

In this program, you analyze how organizations function and where processes can be optimized. You translate business problems into IT solutions and guide their implementation. Sometimes you develop solutions yourself, and sometimes you collaborate with software developers. You are the connecting link between business and technology.

You work on real-life cases from companies and organizations. Projects are practice-oriented and team-based, helping you develop both technical insight and professional skills such as communication and advising.

---

## WHY BUSINESS IT AND MANAGEMENT?

* You connect business processes with IT solutions.
* You learn to use data and AI to improve organizations.
* You work on real assignments from practice.
* You develop strong analytical and advisory skills.
* You receive personal guidance from experienced lecturers.

---

## WHAT ARE YOU TRAINED FOR?

The Business IT and Management specialization trains you to become a professional who understands both organizational processes and IT systems. You learn how to analyze information flows, improve business processes, and advise organizations on digital innovation.

You are trained to work as a bridge between management, users, and IT specialists, with a strong focus on data-driven decision-making.

---

## POSSIBLE PROFESSIONS AFTER THIS PROGRAM

* **Information Analyst:** You analyze business processes and information flows and propose improvements.
* **BI / Data Consultant:** You help organizations turn data into valuable insights.
* **IT Project Manager:** You manage IT projects and coordinate teams.
* **AI Business Consultant:** You translate organizational needs into AI-based solutions.
* **Business IT Consultant:** You advise organizations on effective use of IT systems.

---

## WHAT IS EXPECTED OF YOU?

You choose Business IT and Management because you are curious about how organizations work and how technology can support them. You enjoy analyzing problems, thinking creatively, and working with data. You are communicative and like collaborating with different stakeholders.

You are interested in both people and technology. Prior programming knowledge is not required.

---

## FURTHER EDUCATION

After completing this specialization, you can continue with a 1 or 2-year Master’s program at a university of applied sciences or a research university.

Master’s programs that align well include:

* Chain Information Systems
* Strategic Information Management
* ERP and Logistics

You can also choose the HBO Top Program transfer minor to gain faster access to a university Master’s program.

## CURRICULUM (DEN BOSCH)

**Study structure**
You work in periods of 10 weeks on a central theme. Each period includes a group project supported by courses and practical assignments. You also develop professional skills such as communication, project management, and research.

**Year 1**
Introduction to IT and business processes. You learn how organizations use IT and work on real company cases.
Important subjects include:

* Business Administration
* Information & Process Analysis
* Databases
* Communication and Reporting

**Year 2**
You deepen your knowledge of information systems and data-driven solutions.
Important subjects include:

* ERP
* Business Intelligence
* Low-Code Development
* Programming

**Year 3**
You complete a half-year internship and focus on data analytics and applied research.
Important components:

* Internship
* Data Analytics
* Elective module

**Year 4**
You graduate by independently working on an IT-related business problem. You complete a final project and an elective module.
